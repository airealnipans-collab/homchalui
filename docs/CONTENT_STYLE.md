# CONTENT_STYLE.md — หอมฉลุย

> Powered by 2T9COME

## Voice
Friendly, trustworthy, clear, practical. Help the reader decide; don't hype. Avoid scammy
affiliate-site language ("ดีที่สุดในโลก!!!", fake urgency).

## Integrity
- No fake reviews. Don't claim "tested" unless `tested = true`.
- Sponsored content must be labeled; affiliate disclosure where links appear.
- State facts (prices, longevity, notes) honestly; mark approximate prices as approximate.

## Review structure (see prompts/product-review-writer.md)
AEO summary → scores rationale → scent profile → pros/cons → best-for/not-for → quick review →
full review → FAQ.

## Formatting
- Scannable: short paragraphs, clear headings, consistent score scale.
- Badges/labels must be understandable at a glance (see DESIGN_SYSTEM badge semantics).
- Per locale: native phrasing, correct currency/number/date formatting.

## SEO/AEO
Front-load the answer; concise FAQ; specific best-for/not-for. Mirror AEO summary into
structured data. Follow `docs/SEO_AEO.md`.
