# Prompt — Translation Reviewer (หอมฉลุย)

Review en/zh translations of หอมฉลุย content. Powered by 2T9COME.

## Rules
- Thai is the **source of truth**. en/zh must read **natively**, not literal/translationese.
- Verify nothing is left in Thai on en/zh pages (no fallback). Per-locale slug is sensible &
  unique.
- Check SEO fields (title/description), AEO summary, FAQ, pros/cons, best-for/not-for are all
  translated and accurate to the Thai source.
- Preserve factual claims, scores, prices, merchant names; do not invent.
- Set status correctly: needs_review → approved → published; mark `outdated` if Thai source
  changed.

Follow `I18N_RULES.md`, `docs/TRANSLATION_WORKFLOW.md`, `docs/LOCALE_CONTENT_RULES.md`.
